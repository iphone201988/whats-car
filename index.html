Start Your Engines…

In the fast-paced world of recruitment and relationships, understanding someone’s personality can be as essential as checking under the hood of a high-performance car. Inspired by Carl Jung’s research into psychological types, we have created a new spin on personality profiling, one that blends Jung’s deep insights with a modern new approach called (CAR) Characteristics, Attitude, and Results.

Imagine human personality as the engine that powers everything we do, with each person reflecting a different model of thinking, feeling, and acting. And just like with cars, you can tell a lot about someone by the way they drive through life. The CAR framework is rooted in Jung’s work, and applies to human personalities, with each personality type symbolized by an iconic vehicle. Because at the end of the day, we’re all navigating the twists and turns of life in our unique way.
